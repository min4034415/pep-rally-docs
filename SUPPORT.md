## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I navigate between screens in Dog Mode?**
Swipe left or right to move between pages in Dog Mode. The pages are:
1. **Walk Timer** — live timer with map showing your route
2. **QR Dog Tag** — tap "Show QR Code" to display owner contact info
3. **Night Shine** — solid color night visibility screen, swipe to cycle through colors
4. **Night Shine Symbol** — symbol-based visibility screen, tap to cycle through symbols

**How do I end the walk in Dog Mode?**
Tap the red X button in the top right corner. You'll be asked to confirm before the session ends.

**How do I pause the walk in Dog Mode?**
Tap the pause button on the bottom toolbar. Tap it again to resume.

**How do I change the Night Shine color?**
On the Night Shine page, swipe left or right to cycle through available colors. Your last selected color is remembered for next time.

**How do I show the QR dog tag?**
Swipe to the QR Dog Tag page in Dog Mode and tap "Show QR Code". Make sure you've set your owner contact info first in Settings > Owner Contact.

**The app isn't tracking my dog's walk correctly.**
Make sure location and motion permissions are enabled in Settings > Privacy & Security. Place your iPhone securely in your dog's harness pouch for best results.

**The map shows the wrong location.**
Make sure location permission is set to "While Using the App" in Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Pep Rally.

**How do I add another dog?**
Go to Settings > Add Dog Profile.

**How do I delete a dog profile?**
Go to Settings > Dog Profile, scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Dog Profile".

**The Night Shine screen isn't working.**
Night Shine uses Metal graphics. Make sure your device supports Metal (iPhone 6 and later). If issues persist, try restarting the app.

**Walk sessions aren't saving.**
Make sure you tap the end button to properly finish a session. Check Settings > Walk History to view all past sessions.

## Requirements
